Wretched Beast

  • Molasses
  • Guacamol√©
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Sesame Seeds
  • 2lbs Tofu
Instrucitionsnis
  1. Chop cilantro and slice lime
  2. Heat pan for tofu
  3. While heating pan, chop garlic and ginger
  4. Mix chopped ingredients in a bowl with tofu and cilantro. Combine with molasses.
  5. 4 cups of guacamole combine in bowl
  6. Saute over medium heat, lightly flambe over 30 seconds, and serve
  7. Garnish w/ pumpkin pie spice and sesame seeds.
