Frequently Asked Questions

Accesibility

  • We offer an allergy friendly menu option, as well as gluten free options. If you have any specific dietary needs please let your server know or include it in your booking.
  • Our restaurant is wheelchair and stroller accesible.
  • If you need any additional accomodation for hearing, vision, or other impairments please let the host know as you are seated.

The Menu Mystery

  • While we will always notify you of allergens we do not reveal the mystery ingredients until you guess them properly.
  • Our menu is always updating, so while you may know what it was last time, that's never a guarentee.

Special Events and Parties

  • We host regular events like Drag Night every month working with local artists. Check out our social media to learn more.
  • We can host birthday parties and private events but booking is always limited. To be safe please contact us at least 3 weeks prior to the event date.
  • Any parties of 15 and above will have a 12% gratuity and service fee added to their bill.
