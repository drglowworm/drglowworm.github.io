Daniel Garcia's

The Mint Box

A new design for an age-old plot... and more.
A spectator selects and signs a card. WHAM! It's no longer in the deck! Open up the Altoids box, and show them the folded card inside. The spectator sees you remove and hand them the card to reopen - it's their signed card!

Tannen's

Wonder Needle

The Wonder Needle is an easy to learn miracle that packs small and carries a huge impact on any audience.
Start by handing your spectator a needle and a piece of thread. Ask them to thread the needle and hold on to both ends of the thread. As long they don't let go of the ends, there should be no way for the needle to escape without breaking the thread. Under cover of a handkerchief, you proceed to do exactly that and pull the needle free from the thread without damaging the needle or the thread, and everything is left completely examinable!

The Magic Smith's

Double Cross

Ask a spectator to hold out their hand and make a closed fist. Pull out a marker and draw an 'X' on the center of your palm and close it into a fist. Now without any rubbing, erasing or funny business, you slowly open your hand and reveal your X is COMPLETELY GONE. Now ask the spectator to open their hand - THE X IS ON THEIR PALM!
